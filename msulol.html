My Experience

I've been playing LOL for MSU's teams for a few months, as a substitute player for the main team and a player for the JV team. It's been a very demanding but cool experience, and I've met a lot of really nice people. I've played LoL since I was 12 and I really love the game, so being able to play with a consistent team of people who also love the competition has been a lot of fun for me. I'm currently working on improving my own play, since I'd like to play for the main team during 2025/2026, my Senior year, but I'm not good enough yet. I hope that I'll be able to achieve a high level of play, regardless of which team I end up playing for, and it's been an awesome privilege to play tournaments and work on improving with other likeminded people.
